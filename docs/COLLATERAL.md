## Identity system

The refined mark preserves the original open local trust boundary. Two incoming
rails represent the two questions Free2PA answers before load: whether these are
the exact signed bytes and whether this verifier admits the publisher. The rails
converge at a decision point inside the open boundary. The geometry remains
legible at application-icon and gallery-thumbnail sizes.

Primary colors:

- Ink: `#090d0a`
- Paper: `#f2f4f1`
- Verification green: `#42dc8d`
- Warning amber: `#f3b84b`
- Rejection red: `#ff6b68`
- Context blue: `#76a9ff`
