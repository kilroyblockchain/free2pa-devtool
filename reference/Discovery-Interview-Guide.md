# Discovery Interview Guide

## Purpose
Validate real business pain points before building assistant logic.

## Ask These Questions
1. What tasks consume the most time weekly?
2. What tasks are repetitive?
3. What tasks cause frequent mistakes?
4. What tasks would you automate first?
5. What tasks must stay human-controlled?
6. What triggers manager escalation?

## Submission Requirements
- source type (interview or research)
- top 5 validated pain points
- 3 automatable tasks
- 2 human-only tasks
- 1 risk to avoid
