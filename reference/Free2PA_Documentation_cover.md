## Abstract

Free2PA provides a minimal, cryptographically verifiable method for protecting digital skill files and other artifacts without requiring centralized authorities or paid infrastructure.  It enables students, creators, and small teams to establish decentralized trust networks, exchange verifiable artifacts, and enforce runtime safety through a modular pass/fail verification pipeline. The system forms the trust foundation for educational agent frameworks, media provenance demonstrations, and physical-digital custody credential systems.
